## 6. Battle & date ladder (revise in 60 seconds)

- ⚠️ **1191/1192 Tarain** (Prithviraj win then loss) → **1206** Sultanate founded.
- ⚠️ **1296–1316 Alauddin** (price control) → **1325–51 Muhammad bin Tughlaq** (token currency + Daulatabad) → **1398 Timur**.
- ⚠️ **1526 Panipat I** (Babur) → **1527 Khanwa** → **1556 Panipat II** (Akbar) → **1576 Haldighati**.
- ⚠️ **1565 Talikota** (Vijayanagara falls) · **1582 Din-i-Ilahi** · **1679 jizyah** reimposed.
- ⚠️ **1674 Shivaji crowned** → **1707 Aurangzeb dies** → **1761 Panipat III** (Marathas vs Abdali).

> 🔑 **Trap:** **First** Battle of Tarain (1191) = Prithviraj **wins**; **Second** (1192) = Prithviraj **loses**. And **Panipat I = 1526 (Babur vs Ibrahim Lodi)**, **Panipat II = 1556 (Akbar/Bairam Khan vs Hemu)**, **Panipat III = 1761 (Marathas vs Abdali)** — don't mix them up.
