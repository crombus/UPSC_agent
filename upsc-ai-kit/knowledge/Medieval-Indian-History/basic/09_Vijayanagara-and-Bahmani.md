## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — two Deccan powers and three contested zones.**

✅ Vijayanagara and Bahmani kingdoms dominated south of the Vindhyas for more than two centuries. Their conflict centred on the **Tungabhadra doab**, the **Krishna-Godavari basin**, and the **Konkan/Goa coast**.
- ✅ The Tungabhadra doab was fertile and strategically placed between the two powers.
- ✅ The Krishna-Godavari basin had fertile land and ports controlling regional foreign trade.
- ✅ Goa/Konkan mattered because horses from Iran and Iraq were essential to southern armies.

**Foundation — high points and breakdown.**

✅ Vijayanagara became wealthy and powerful under Deva Raya II; foreign travellers such as Nicolo Conti and Abdur Razzaq described its size, population, cultivation and splendour.
- ✅ Deva Raya II strengthened the army by employing Muslim cavalrymen and archers and by learning from Sultanate military methods.
- ✅ Mahmud Gawan expanded Bahmani power, took Goa and Dabhol, and reformed administration.
- ✅ Krishnadeva Raya restored internal order, defeated rivals, patronised literature and built tanks/towns; after his death factional politics and Rama Raja's diplomacy led to the 1565 catastrophe.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Vijayanagara founded **1336**; Bahmani founded **1347**.
- ✅ Main dispute = **Tungabhadra doab**, plus Krishna-Godavari basin and Konkan/Goa.
- ✅ Deva Raya I built a dam across Tungabhadra; canals increased revenue.
- ✅ Deva Raya II reorganised the army and employed Muslim cavalry/archers.
- ✅ Mahmud Gawan captured/controlled western coastal areas including **Goa and Dabhol**.
- ✅ Battle of Talikota/Rakshasa-Tangadi/Bannihatti = **1565**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Vijayanagara-Bahmani conflict was not simply religious; horses, ports, fertile doabs and regional strategy mattered.

- ❌ Vijayanagara never used Muslim soldiers. → Deva Raya II deliberately employed Muslim cavalrymen/archers.
- ❌ Talikota immediately ended every remnant of Vijayanagara. → It ended the great age; the kingdom lingered with shrinking territory.
- ❌ Bahmani decline was due only to Vijayanagara pressure. → Internal Deccani-Afaqi factionalism was crucial.
- ❌ Goa mattered only after the Portuguese. → It was already vital because of horse imports and coastal trade.
