## How this folder works

- **Two tiers per topic:** `basic/NN` builds the must-do entitlement, law, scheme
  and access framework; `advanced/NN` adds implementation gaps, legal nuance,
  institutional trade-offs, data interpretation and Mains depth.
- **Tagging:** ✅ = directly grounded in an official source or verified PYQ;
  ⚠️ = analytical inference or standard linkage; 📰 = a dated current legal,
  scheme or outcome-data anchor.
- **Date discipline:** survey indicators, scheme coverage, crime data, budget
  allocations and portal totals must retain their reporting year/date.
- **Legal discipline:** an enacted Act, notified rule, pending Bill, judicial
  direction and policy recommendation are not interchangeable.

## Boundaries with other subjects

- **Polity owns constitutional doctrine and constitutional bodies:** equality,
  reservation doctrine, Fifth/Sixth Schedules and NCSC/NCST/NCBC composition.
  Social Justice studies how protective law and welfare delivery affect vulnerable
  groups. NCW, NCM and NCPCR are treated here because their statutory protective
  roles are not covered by a dedicated Polity file.
- **Governance owns generic delivery machinery:** policy design, DBT/PFMS,
  NGOs/SHGs, grievance redress, social audit and monitoring/evaluation. Social
  Justice applies these mechanisms to entitlements and group-specific outcomes.
- **Economy owns macro measurement and production systems:** poverty/inequality
  measurement, procurement/buffer stocks, labour-market trends and fiscal policy.
  Social Justice owns access, protection, entitlements and welfare outcomes.

## Study sequence

1. **Foundation:** `01-02` — justice framework and basic deprivation.
2. **Social-sector capabilities:** `03-04` — health, education and human resources.
3. **Group-specific protection:** `05-13` — women, children, caste, tribe,
   backward classes, minorities, disability, ageing and identity-based exclusion.
4. **Precarious work and habitation:** `14-16` — sanitation labour, informal work,
   migration, homelessness and urban poverty.
5. **Synthesis:** `17` — targeting, convergence, outcome data and correction.
