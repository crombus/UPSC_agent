## How this folder works

- **Two tiers per topic:** `basic/NN` builds concepts, mechanisms and direct PYQ use;
  `advanced/NN` adds causal chains, boundary cases, trade-offs and answer frameworks.
- **Tagging:** ✅ = source-grounded or verified PYQ; ⚠️ = analytical inference;
  📰 = dated current anchor. Dates belong with every dynamic statistic.
- **Scope discipline:** This is a sociological module: social structure, social change,
  exclusion and cohesion. It does not duplicate legal doctrine, schemes or macro models.

## Boundaries with other subjects

- **Social-Justice** owns welfare laws, schemes and entitlements; this folder owns the
  social structure and phenomenon beneath them.
- **Geography** owns DTM, migration theory, settlement models and spatial cultural
  regions; this folder owns population, migration and urbanisation as social issues.
- **Economy** owns poverty measurement and macro data; this folder owns poverty as
  social exclusion and deprivation.
- **Polity** owns constitutional doctrine, reservation, federalism and constitutional
  bodies; this folder owns their social effects. Do not re-derive doctrine here.
- **History** owns chronological reform and communalism narratives; this folder owns
  present-day sociological frames. **Philosophy** owns thinker-heavy secularism theory.
