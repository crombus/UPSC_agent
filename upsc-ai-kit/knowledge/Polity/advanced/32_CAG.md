# THE BIG PICTURE ⭐
✅ **Art 148** provides an independent **CAG** — head of the **Indian Audit & Accounts Department**, the
**"guardian of the public purse."** **Dr. Ambedkar** called the CAG **"the most important officer under the
Constitution."** One of the **bulwarks of democracy** (along with the **Supreme Court, Election Commission,
UPSC**).

---

# PART A — APPOINTMENT, TERM & REMOVAL ⭐
| Point | Detail |
|---|---|
| ✅ Appointed by | **President** (warrant under hand & seal) |
| ✅ Term | **6 years or 65 years**, whichever earlier |
| ✅ Removal | Same manner as a **Supreme Court judge** (special-majority resolution of both Houses, proved misbehaviour/incapacity) — **not** during the President's pleasure |
| ✅ Resignation | To the President |

---

# PART B — INDEPENDENCE ⭐
- ✅ **Security of tenure** (removed only by the constitutional procedure).
- ✅ **NOT eligible for any further office** under the Centre or a state after his term (stricter than UPSC).
- ✅ Salary = a **Supreme Court judge**; determined by Parliament; can't be varied to disadvantage.
- ✅ Salaries/expenses of the CAG's office are **charged on the Consolidated Fund of India** (non-votable).
- ✅ **No minister** can represent the CAG in Parliament or take responsibility for his actions.

---

# PART C — DUTIES & POWERS (Art 149–151) ⭐
✅ **CAG's (DPC) Act, 1971** (amended 1976 — separated **accounts from audit**). The CAG audits:
- All expenditure from the **Consolidated Fund** of India / states / UTs-with-legislature.
- **Contingency Fund & Public Account** of India and states.
- Trading/manufacturing/P&L/balance-sheet accounts of departments.
- **Receipts & expenditure**; **bodies substantially financed** from govt revenue; **government companies** & other
  corporations (per law).
- ✅ **Advises the President** on the **form of accounts** (Art 150).
- ✅ Submits audit reports on **Centre → President → Parliament (Art 151)**; on a **state → Governor → State
  Legislature**.
- ✅ Ascertains & certifies the **net proceeds** of any tax (Art 279) — certificate is **final**.
- ✅ **Guide, friend & philosopher of the Public Accounts Committee (PAC)**.

✅ **Three audit reports** to the President: (1) **Appropriation Accounts** (actual vs sanctioned expenditure),
(2) **Finance Accounts** (annual receipts & disbursements), (3) **Public Undertakings**.

---

# PART D — ROLE, TYPES OF AUDIT & LIMITATIONS ⭐⭐
- ✅ CAG is an **agent of Parliament**, responsible **only to Parliament** — secures executive accountability.
- ✅ **Legal/regulatory audit** (was money legally available & properly spent?) is **obligatory**; **propriety
  audit** (wisdom, faithfulness, economy — wastefulness/extravagance) is **discretionary**.
- ⚠️ **Comptroller in name only:** unlike Britain's CAG, India's CAG has **no control over the issue of money** from
  the Consolidated Fund — he audits **after** the expenditure (Auditor-General role only, **not** Comptroller).
- ✅ **Secret service expenditure** is a **limitation** — he accepts a certificate from the competent authority, not
  particulars.
- ⚠️ **Appleby's criticism:** wanted the CAG's office abolished — called auditing "repressive/negative," a colonial
  inheritance.

## CAG & public corporations (3 categories) ✅
(i) **Audited fully by CAG** (e.g., ONGC, Air India, DVC); (ii) **private auditors + CAG supplementary audit**
(e.g., IFC); (iii) **exclusively private audit** — CAG not involved (e.g., **RBI, LIC, SBI, FCI**).

## UPSC Traps
- ❌ The CAG both controls and audits the Consolidated Fund → in practice he is **only Auditor-General** (no
  comptrolling power, unlike Britain).
- ❌ CAG audits state accounts and reports to Parliament → state reports go to the **Governor → State Legislature**.
- ❌ CAG can take another government job after retirement → **barred** from any further Centre/state office.
- ❌ Propriety audit is compulsory → it is **discretionary** (only legal/regulatory audit is obligatory).
- ❌ CAG audits RBI/LIC/SBI → these are **exclusively private-audited** (CAG not involved).
- ❌ CAG's term is fixed at 6 years only → **6 years or 65**, whichever earlier.
- ❌ CAG is removed by the President at pleasure → removed **only** like a Supreme Court judge.
