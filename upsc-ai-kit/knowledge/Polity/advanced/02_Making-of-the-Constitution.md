## 1. Demand for a Constituent Assembly
✅ **1934** — idea first floated by **M.N. Roy** (communist pioneer). **1935** — **INC** officially
demanded it. **1938** — Nehru: Constitution of free India must be framed by a CA elected on **adult
franchise**, without outside interference. ✅ Accepted in principle in the **August Offer, 1940**.
✅ **Cripps Mission (1942)** — draft for post-WWII Constitution; rejected by **Muslim League** (wanted
two Constituent Assemblies). ✅ **Cabinet Mission (1946)** — rejected two CAs but gave a scheme.

## 2. Composition (Cabinet Mission Plan, Nov 1946)
✅ Total strength **389**: **296 British India** (292 from 11 governors' provinces + 4 from chief
commissioners' provinces) + **93 princely states**. **~1 seat per million** population. Provincial
seats split among **Muslims, Sikhs, General** by population; elected by respective community members
of provincial assemblies via **proportional representation / single transferable vote**. Princely-
state reps **nominated** by rulers → CA was **partly elected + partly nominated**, and **indirectly
elected**.

✅ **1946 elections (296 seats):** INC **208**, Muslim League **73**, others **15**. 93 princely-
state seats initially unfilled. CA represented all sections (Hindus, Muslims, Sikhs, Parsis,
Anglo-Indians, Christians, SCs, STs, women) — **except Mahatma Gandhi** (not a member).

## 3. Working
✅ First meeting **9 Dec 1946**; Muslim League **boycotted** (211 attended). **Dr. Sachchidananda
Sinha** = temporary President (oldest member, French practice). Later **Dr. Rajendra Prasad** =
President; **two Vice-Presidents** (H.C. Mukherjee & V.T. Krishnamachari).

### Objectives Resolution ⭐
✅ Moved by **Nehru on 13 Dec 1946**; laid down philosophy & fundamentals. Adopted **22 Jan 1947**.
Its **modified version = the Preamble**. Key ideas: India as **Independent Sovereign Republic**;
Union of territories with autonomous units + residuary powers; **power derived from the people**;
justice/equality/freedom to all; **safeguards for minorities, backward & tribal areas**; territorial
integrity; world peace.

## 4. Changes by the Independence Act, 1947
✅ (1) CA made a **fully sovereign body** — could frame any constitution & repeal British Acts.
(2) CA also became a **legislative body** (first Parliament / Dominion Legislature) — as constituent
body chaired by **Rajendra Prasad**, as legislative body by **G.V. Mavlankar** (till 26 Nov 1949).
(3) Muslim League members (Pakistan areas) withdrew → strength **389 → 299** (provinces 296→229,
states 93→70).

## 5. Other functions performed
✅ Ratified **Commonwealth membership (May 1949)**; adopted **National Flag (22 Jul 1947)**, **National
Anthem & National Song (24 Jan 1950)**; elected **Rajendra Prasad first President (24 Jan 1950)**.
✅ **11 sessions** over **2 years, 11 months, 18 days**; studied ~**60 countries'** constitutions;
draft debated **114 days**; cost **₹64 lakh**. Continued as **provisional parliament** from 26 Jan
1950 until the first Parliament (1951–52 elections).

## 7. Drafting Committee (set up 29 Aug 1947) — 7 members ⭐
✅ (1) **Dr. B.R. Ambedkar (Chairman)**, (2) N. Gopalaswamy Ayyangar, (3) Alladi Krishnaswamy Ayyar,
(4) K.M. Munshi, (5) Syed Mohammad Saadullah, (6) **N. Madhava Rau** (replaced B.L. Mitter, resigned),
(7) **T.T. Krishnamachari** (replaced D.P. Khaitan, died 1948). Sat only **141 days**; first draft
**Feb 1948**, second draft **Oct 1948**.

## 8. Enactment
✅ Ambedkar introduced final draft **4 Nov 1948** (1st reading). 2nd reading (clause-by-clause)
15 Nov 1948 – 17 Oct 1949 (**7653 amendments proposed, 2473 discussed**). 3rd reading from 14 Nov
1949; **passed 26 Nov 1949** — signed by **284 of 299** present. As adopted: **Preamble + 395 Articles
+ 8 Schedules** (Preamble enacted **last**). ✅ **Ambedkar = "Father of the Constitution" / "Modern
Manu."**

## 9. Enforcement
✅ Some Articles (citizenship, elections, provisional parliament etc. — **Art 5,6,7,8,9,60,324,366,
367,379,380,388,391,392,393**) came into force **26 Nov 1949**. **Rest on 26 Jan 1950 = "date of
commencement" / Republic Day.** ✅ **26 Jan** chosen for historical value — **Purna Swaraj Day (1930)**
(Lahore Session, Dec 1929). On commencement, the Independence Act 1947 & GoI Act 1935 were **repealed**
(Abolition of Privy Council Jurisdiction Act 1949 continued).

## 10. Congress Experts Committee (8 Jul 1946)
✅ Chaired by **Nehru** (members: Asaf Ali, Munshi, Ayyangar, K.T. Shah, D.R. Gadgil, Humayun Kabir,
K. Santhanam; convener Krishna Kripalani). Granville Austin: "**It set India on the road to her
present Constitution**"; drafted a resolution resembling the Objectives Resolution.

## 11. Criticism of the CA
✅ (1) **Not representative** (no direct adult-franchise election); (2) **not sovereign** (British-
created); (3) **time-consuming** (US took 4 months; Naziruddin Ahmed called it a **"Drifting
Committee"**); (4) **Congress-dominated** (Austin: "Assembly was the Congress and the Congress was
India"); (5) **lawyer-politician dominated**; (6) **Hindu-dominated** (Viscount Simon: "a body of
Hindus"; Churchill: "only one major community").

## 12. Important facts (Prelims gold)
✅ Symbol/seal = **Elephant**; Constitutional/legal advisor = **Sir B.N. Rau**; Secretary = **H.V.R.
Iyengar**; chief draftsman = **S.N. Mukerjee**; calligrapher = **Prem Behari Narain Raizada** (flowing
italic, hand-written); art by **Shantiniketan** (Nand Lal Bose; Preamble by **Beohar Rammanohar
Sinha**). ✅ Authoritative **Hindi text** provided by **58th Amendment, 1987**.

## UPSC Traps
- ❌ CA directly elected on adult franchise → **indirectly** elected, partly nominated.
- ❌ Constitution adopted 26 Jan 1950 → **adopted 26 Nov 1949**, **commenced 26 Jan 1950**.
- ❌ Temporary President was Rajendra Prasad → **Sachchidananda Sinha**.
- ❌ Objectives Resolution moved by Ambedkar → **Nehru** (13 Dec 1946).
- ❌ Original Constitution had a Preamble drafted first → **Preamble enacted last**.
- ❌ Gandhi was a CA member → **not a member**.
- ❌ Drafting Committee had 5 members → **7**.
