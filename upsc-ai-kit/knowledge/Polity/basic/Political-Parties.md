## Core idea
India has a **multi-party system** (largest in the world) that evolved from the **"Congress system"** to competitive
coalition politics. The **ECI** grants **national/state** recognition based on **poll performance**, which brings a
**reserved symbol** and other privileges.

> 🔑 **National party** — any one of: **6% in 4+ states + 4 LS seats** · **2% LS seats from 3 states** · **state party
> in 4 states**.
> 🔑 Recognised parties get a **reserved symbol** + **40 star campaigners**; unrecognised pick a **free symbol** + 20.

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Registered under **Section 29A of the RPA, 1951**.
- ✅ Rajni Kothari → **"one-party dominance / Congress system."**
- ✅ Recognition privileges: reserved symbol, broadcast time, one proposer, electoral-roll access.
