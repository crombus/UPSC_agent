## 1. Snapshot

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Part of Constitution | ✅ Part IV (Articles 36–51) |

| Nature | ✅ **Non-justiciable** (courts can't enforce) |
| Duty clause | ✅ Art 37 — "fundamental in governance"; State's duty to apply |

## 2. Core idea

✅ DPSP are **ideals/instructions to the State** while making laws and policies. They aim to build a
**welfare state** and secure **social & economic democracy** (whereas FRs secure **political
democracy**). Not legally enforceable, but morally & politically binding — the real force is
**public opinion**.

## 3. The 3 categories (Laxmikant's classification)

| Category | Idea | Example articles |
|---|---|---|
| 🟥 Socialistic | social-economic justice, welfare | ✅ 38, 39, 39A, 41, 42, 43, 43A, 47 |
| 🟩 Gandhian | rural reconstruction | ✅ 40 (panchayats), 43 (cottage industry), 43B (co-ops), 46, 47 (prohibition), 48 (cow slaughter) |
| 🟦 Liberal-Intellectual | modern liberal state | ✅ 44 (UCC), 45, 48 (agri), 48A (environment), 49, 50 (separate judiciary), 51 (world peace) |

> 🔑 Mnemonic (Gandhian): **"PC-CoW"** → Panchayats, Cottage industry, Co-operatives,
> Weaker-sections, alcohol prohibition, cow protection.

## 5. Amendments that added DPSP

- ✅ **42nd (1976):** Art 39(f), 39A (legal aid), 43A (workers in management), 48A (environment).
- ✅ **44th (1978):** Art 38 — minimise inequalities.
- ✅ **86th (2002):** Art 45 changed → elementary education became FR (Art 21A).
- ✅ **97th (2011):** Art 43B — co-operative societies.

## 6. FR vs DPSP (one-line)

| Fundamental Rights | Directive Principles |
|---|---|
| ✅ Justiciable | ✅ Non-justiciable |
| Political democracy | Social & economic democracy |
| Negative (limit State) | Positive (direct State to act) |

## 7. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ DPSP = Articles **36–51**, Part IV; borrowed from **Ireland**.
- ✅ **Non-justiciable** (Art 37); no court enforcement.
- ✅ Art **44** = UCC; Art **40** = panchayats; Art **50** = separate judiciary.
- ✅ **Present position:** FR prevail over DPSP, but Parliament can amend FR to implement DPSP without
  damaging **basic structure** (Minerva Mills, 1980).
