## 2. Core idea

✅ The Preamble is the **introduction/essence** of the Constitution. It declares India a **Sovereign
Socialist Secular Democratic Republic** and sets out four objectives: **Justice, Liberty, Equality,
Fraternity**.

## 3. Four ingredients

1. ✅ **Source of authority** — the people of India.
2. ✅ **Nature of state** — sovereign, socialist, secular, democratic, republic.
3. ✅ **Objectives** — justice, liberty, equality, fraternity.
4. ✅ **Date** — 26 November 1949.

## 4. Key words — meaning + origin

| Word | Quick meaning | Origin |
|---|---|---|
| ✅ Sovereign | Independent | — |
| ✅ Socialist | Democratic socialism (mixed economy); added 1976 | — |
| ✅ Secular | Equal respect to all religions; added 1976 | — |
| ✅ Democratic | Rule by the people (representative) | — |
| ✅ Republic | Elected head (President) | — |
| ✅ **Justice** | Social, economic, political | **Russian Revolution** |
| ✅ **Liberty · Equality · Fraternity** | Freedom, no privileges, brotherhood | **French Revolution** |

> 🔑 Origins trap: **Justice = Russian Revolution (1917)** · **Liberty/Equality/Fraternity = French
> Revolution (1789)**.

## 5. Preamble & the courts

| Case | Ruling |
|---|---|
| ✅ Berubari Union (1960) | Preamble **not** a part of the Constitution |
| ✅ Kesavananda Bharati (1973) | Preamble **IS** a part |
| ✅ LIC of India (1995) | Reaffirmed — integral part |

✅ It is **non-justiciable** and neither a source of nor a limit on legislative power. It **can** be
amended (Art 368) but not its basic features.

## 6. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Based on the **Objectives Resolution** (Nehru); enacted **last** by the CA.
- ✅ Amended **only once** — **42nd Amdt 1976** (Socialist, Secular, Integrity).
- ✅ **Justice ← Russian Revolution**; **Liberty/Equality/Fraternity ← French Revolution**.
- ✅ Preamble is **non-justiciable**.

## 7. 📰 Current link

📰 In **Dr. Balram Singh v. Union of India (Nov 2024)**, the Supreme Court **upheld** the words
"socialist" & "secular" in the Preamble, calling the Constitution a "living document."
