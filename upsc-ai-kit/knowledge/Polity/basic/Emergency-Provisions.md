# Emergency Provisions (Art 352–360) — BASIC

> **Subject:** Polity · **Tier:** Basic (foundation) · **GS Paper:** GS-II
> **Grounded in:** Laxmikant Ch. 16, Part XVIII. ✅ = from book · ⚠️ = case law · 📰 = current affairs.
> *Full depth: `advanced/14_Emergency-Provisions.md`.*

---

## Snapshot — 3 emergencies
| Type | Article | Ground |
|---|---|---|
| ✅ **National** | **352** | War / external aggression / **armed rebellion** |
| ✅ **President's Rule** (State) | **356** | Failure of constitutional machinery in a state |
| ✅ **Financial** | **360** | Threat to India's financial stability/credit |

## Core idea
✅ During an emergency the **federal system turns unitary** without amending the Constitution (Ambedkar).
The **44th Amendment (1978)** added most safeguards after the misuse of the **1975 Emergency**.

## National Emergency (352) — key numbers
| Item | Rule |
|---|---|
| ✅ Recommendation | **Cabinet in writing** (not PM alone) |
| ✅ Approval | **1 month**, **special majority** |
| ✅ Duration | 6 months, **indefinite** renewal |
| ✅ Revocation | LS simple-majority disapproval → must revoke |
| ✅ Declared | **1962, 1971, 1975** (3 times) |

✅ **FR effect:** **Art 358** auto-suspends **Art 19** (only in External Emergency); **Art 359** suspends
enforcement of specified FRs — but **Art 20 & 21 can NEVER be suspended**.

## President's Rule (356)
✅ Approval within **2 months, simple majority**; max **3 years**. Beyond 1 year needs a National
Emergency + **ECI certification**. CM/CoM dismissed; assembly suspended/dissolved; **HC untouched**.
> 🔑 **Bommai (1994):** Art 356 is **judicially reviewable**; majority tested on the **floor**;
> **secularism = basic feature**; court can **restore** a dismissed government.

## Financial Emergency (360)
✅ Approval **2 months, simple majority**; then **indefinite** (no renewal). Centre can cut salaries of
state staff **and SC/HC judges**. ✅ **Never declared.**

## Quick contrast
> 🔑 **352 = Special majority + indefinite + affects FRs + LS can revoke.**
> 🔑 **356 = Simple majority + max 3 years + no FR effect + President revokes.**

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ "Internal disturbance" → replaced by "**armed rebellion**" (44th Amdt).
- ✅ **Alladi Krishnaswami Ayyar:** emergency provisions = "**life-breath of the Constitution**."
- ✅ Ambedkar hoped **Art 356** would stay a "**dead letter**" (last resort).
- ✅ Financial Emergency modelled on the US **National Recovery Act 1933**.
