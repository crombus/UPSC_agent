## 1. Snapshot

| Item | Detail |

| Part of Constitution | ✅ Part IV-A (single Article — **51A**) |

## 2. Core idea

✅ FDs remind citizens that alongside rights they owe **duties** to the country, society and fellow
citizens. Added after the internal Emergency (1975–77). They are **moral & civic** obligations —
not enforceable by courts, but Parliament **may** legislate to enforce them.

## 3. The 11 Fundamental Duties (Art 51A) — keyword form

| | Duty (keyword) |
|---|---|
| a | Respect **Constitution, Flag, Anthem** |
| b | Cherish **freedom-struggle ideals** |
| c | Protect **sovereignty, unity, integrity** |
| d | **Defend the country** / national service |
| e | Promote **harmony**; dignity of women |
| f | Preserve **composite culture** |
| g | Protect **environment** & wildlife |
| h | Develop **scientific temper** |
| i | Safeguard **public property**; abjure violence |
| j | Strive for **excellence** |
| k | Provide **education** to child (6–14) — **86th Amdt** |

> 🔑 Mnemonic: the **11th duty (education 6–14)** links with **Art 21A** & **Art 45** — all from the
> **86th Amendment, 2002**.

## 4. Key features

- ✅ Mix of **moral** (noble ideals) and **civic** (respect Flag/Anthem) duties.
- ✅ Apply to **citizens only** (not foreigners).
- ✅ **Non-justiciable** — no direct court enforcement.
- ✅ Swaran Singh Committee recommended **8**; 42nd Amdt added **10**; 86th Amdt made it **11**.

## 5. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ FDs = **Part IV-A, Article 51A**; borrowed from **USSR**.
- ✅ **42nd Amdt (1976)** added them; **86th Amdt (2002)** added the 11th (education).
- ✅ **Swaran Singh Committee** (1976) recommended FDs.
- ✅ Duties to **pay taxes / vote** are **NOT** Fundamental Duties.
- ✅ **Non-justiciable** and for **citizens only**.

## 6. 📰 Current link

📰 In *Durga Dutt v. Union of India* (2024), the Supreme Court said FDs can't be made enforceable by
directing the legislature — enforcement should come through **citizen awareness**, echoing the
**Verma Committee (1999)**.
