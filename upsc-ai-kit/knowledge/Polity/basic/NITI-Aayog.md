## Core idea
NITI Aayog is the government's **premier policy think tank** — it replaced the top-down, fund-allocating Planning
Commission with an **advisory, bottom-up, cooperative-federalism** model. It **recommends** but does **not allocate
funds** (that's the Finance Ministry).

> 🔑 **Planning Commission = allocated funds (top-down); NITI Aayog = advises (bottom-up).**
> 🔑 **4 functions:** policy design · cooperative federalism · monitoring & evaluation · think-tank.

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Chair = **PM**; **Vice-Chair** (PM-appointed, Cabinet-Minister rank); **CEO** (Secretary rank).
- ✅ **Governing Council** = all state CMs + UT (legislature) CMs + LGs of other UTs.
- ✅ Wings: **Research, Consultancy, Team India**.
- ✅ **No fund-allocation power**; only recommends.
- ✅ Five-Year Plans ended (**12th Plan 2012–17**) → replaced by 3-yr Action Agenda / 7-yr Strategy / 15-yr Vision.
