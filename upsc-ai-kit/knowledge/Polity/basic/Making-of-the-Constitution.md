## 2. Core idea

✅ The Constituent Assembly (CA) framed the Constitution. It was **indirectly elected** (by provincial
assemblies), **partly nominated** (princely states), and dominated by the **INC**. It took
**2 years, 11 months, 18 days** across **11 sessions**.

## 4. Objectives Resolution

✅ Moved by **Nehru (13 Dec 1946)**, adopted **22 Jan 1947**. It laid down the philosophy of the
Constitution — its **modified form became the Preamble**.

## 5. Drafting Committee (7 members)

✅ Original committee: **Ambedkar (Chair)**, N. Gopalaswamy Ayyangar, Alladi
Krishnaswamy Ayyar, K.M. Munshi, Syed Mohammad Saadullah, **B.L. Mitter** and
**D.P. Khaitan**. N. Madhava Rau replaced Mitter; T.T. Krishnamachari replaced Khaitan.
The committee sat for **141 days**.

## 6. Key dates

- ✅ **9 Dec 1946** — first meeting.
- ✅ **26 Nov 1949** — Constitution **adopted** (Preamble + 395 Articles + 8 Schedules).
- ✅ **26 Jan 1950** — Constitution **commenced** (chosen to honour **Purna Swaraj Day, 1930**).

> 🔑 Remember: **Adopted 26 Nov 1949 · Enforced 26 Jan 1950.** Don't merge the two dates!

## 7. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Idea of CA = **M.N. Roy (1934)**; formed under **Cabinet Mission (1946)**.
- ✅ Temporary President = **Sachchidananda Sinha**; President = **Rajendra Prasad**.
- ✅ **Ambedkar** = Drafting Committee Chairman & "Father of the Constitution."
- ✅ **Gandhi was NOT** a member of the CA.
- ✅ CA symbol = **Elephant**; calligrapher = **Prem Behari Narain Raizada**.
