## Core idea
The GST Council is a **joint Centre-state forum** that takes all major decisions on GST — rates, exemptions, laws
— by a special weighted majority. It is the flagship model of **cooperative federalism**, though its advice is
**not binding** (Mohit Minerals, 2022).

> 🔑 **Voting maths:** decisions need **3/4 weighted votes**; **Centre = 1/3**, **all states = 2/3**; **quorum = ½**.
> 🔑 The Centre alone can **block** (3/4 needed) but **cannot pass** without states.

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Chair = **Union FM**; members = Union MoS Finance + one minister per state; **Vice-Chair chosen by states**.
- ✅ **Revenue Secretary** = ex-officio Secretary; **CBIC chair** = permanent non-voting invitee.
- ✅ Petrol, diesel, ATF, natural gas, crude are **outside GST** until the Council decides a date.
- ✅ Recommended **5-year compensation** to states (ended June 2022).
