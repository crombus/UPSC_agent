## 2. Core idea

✅ Many features of our Constitution have **roots in British-era laws**. A chain of Acts built the
legal framework of government in India — the most important being the **Government of India Act, 1935**
(the biggest source of the present Constitution).

## 3. Company Rule — key Acts

| Act | Landmark |
|---|---|
| ✅ Regulating Act **1773** | GG of **Bengal** (Warren Hastings); Supreme Court Calcutta |
| ✅ Pitt's India Act **1784** | Board of Control → **double government** |
| ✅ Charter Act **1813** | Ended Company trade monopoly (except tea/China) |
| ✅ Charter Act **1833** | GG of **India** (William Bentinck); centralisation |
| ✅ Charter Act **1853** | Separated legislative & executive; open competition for civil service |

## 4. Crown Rule — key Acts

| Act | Landmark |
|---|---|
| ✅ GoI Act **1858** | Abolished Company; **Viceroy** (Canning); Secretary of State |
| ✅ Councils Act **1861** | Indians in law-making; portfolio system |
| ✅ Councils Act **1909** (Morley-Minto) | **Separate electorate for Muslims** (Minto = father of communal electorate) |
| ✅ GoI Act **1919** (Montagu-Chelmsford) | **Dyarchy** in provinces; bicameralism; direct elections |
| ✅ GoI Act **1935** | **Provincial autonomy**; federal scheme; RBI; Federal Court |
| ✅ Independence Act **1947** | Two dominions; end of British rule |

> 🔑 Mnemonic (reforms pairs): **1909 = communal electorate** · **1919 = dyarchy** ·
> **1935 = provincial autonomy**. (Don't swap them!)

## 5. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ First **GG of Bengal** = Warren Hastings (1773); first **GG of India** = William Bentinck (1833).
- ✅ First **Viceroy** = Lord Canning (1858).
- ✅ **Separate electorate for Muslims** = Councils Act **1909**.
- ✅ **Dyarchy** in provinces = GoI Act **1919**; abolished by **1935** (provincial autonomy).
- ✅ **GoI Act 1935** = 321 Sections, 10 Schedules; biggest source of the Constitution.
- ✅ **Poona Pact (1932)** followed the Communal Award (SC reserved seats, joint electorate).
