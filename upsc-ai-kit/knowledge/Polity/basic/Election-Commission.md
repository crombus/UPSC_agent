## Core idea
The **Election Commission of India (ECI)** conducts free & fair elections to **Parliament, State Legislatures,
and the President/Vice-President offices** (NOT local bodies — those are the State Election Commission). The CEC
and two ECs have **equal powers** and decide differences by **majority**.

> 🔑 **EC powers = ABC** — **A**dministrative · **A**dvisory · **Quasi-Judicial**.
> 🔑 **Removal asymmetry:** CEC removed like an SC judge; **other ECs only on the CEC's recommendation**.

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Appointed by the **President**; term **6 yr / 65 yr**.
- ✅ Voting age lowered **21 → 18** by the **61st Amendment (1988)**.
- ✅ **Art 326** = universal adult suffrage; **Art 329** = bar on court interference (election petitions only).
- ✅ Governed by **RPA 1950 & 1951**.
- ✅ Reforms: **NOTA (2013), VVPAT, exit-poll ban, NRI voting**.
