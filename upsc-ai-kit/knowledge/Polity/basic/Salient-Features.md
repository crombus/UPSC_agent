## 2. Core idea

✅ The world's **lengthiest written constitution**, borrowed from many countries but adapted to India.
It is **federal with a unitary bias**, **parliamentary**, **secular**, and has an **integrated,
independent judiciary**.

## 5. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Art 1 = **"Union of States"** (word "Federation" never used).
- ✅ India = **federal with unitary bias** ("quasi-federal" — K.C. Wheare).
- ✅ **Secular/Socialist** added to Preamble by **42nd Amdt, 1976**.
- ✅ Voting age **21 → 18** by **61st Amdt, 1988**.
- ✅ **73rd & 74th Amdts (1992)** = third-tier (panchayats/municipalities).

## 6. 📰 Current link

📰 J&K had its own constitution (Art 370) till 2019; abrogation **upheld by SC in Dec 2023** — a live
illustration of India's federal-with-unitary-bias design.
