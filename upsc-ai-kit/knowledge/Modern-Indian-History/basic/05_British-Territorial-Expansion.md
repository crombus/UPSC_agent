## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — Company expansion used war plus diplomacy.**

- ✅ After Bengal, the Company used revenue, armies and alliances to defeat Mysore, Marathas and Sikhs.
- ✅ Mysore under Haidar Ali and Tipu Sultan was a major southern opponent because it tried to modernise its army and resist Company control.
- ✅ The Maratha confederacy was powerful but divided among the Peshwa, Scindia, Holkar, Bhonsle and Gaekwad.
- ✅ Sikh power remained strong under Ranjit Singh, but after his death internal weakness and Anglo-Sikh wars led to annexation.

**Foundation — two instruments must never be confused.**

- ✅ **Subsidiary Alliance** was associated with Lord Wellesley from **1798**; Indian states accepted British troops, paid subsidy, kept a Resident and gave up independent diplomacy.
- ✅ **Doctrine of Lapse** was associated with Lord Dalhousie; states without a natural heir could be annexed by refusing adoption rights.
- ✅ Awadh was annexed in **1856** on alleged misgovernance, not by Doctrine of Lapse.
- ⚠️ These devices converted Indian rulers into dependent allies before outright annexation.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **First Anglo-Mysore War (1767–69)** ended with the **Treaty of Madras**.
- ✅ **Second Anglo-Mysore War (1780–84)** ended with the **Treaty of Mangalore**; Haidar Ali died in **1782**.
- ✅ **Third Anglo-Mysore War (1790–92)** ended with the **Treaty of Seringapatam**; Tipu ceded half his territory.
- ✅ **Fourth Anglo-Mysore War (1799)**: Tipu Sultan died defending Seringapatam; Wodeyars restored.
- ✅ **First Anglo-Maratha War (1775–82)** ended with the **Treaty of Salbai**.
- ✅ **Second Anglo-Maratha War (1803–05)**: Wellesley; Assaye; Scindia and Bhonsle submitted.
- ✅ **Third Anglo-Maratha War (1817–18)**: Peshwa Baji Rao II deposed and pensioned at Bithur.
- ✅ **Subsidiary Alliance**: Wellesley; first Hyderabad in **1798**.
- ✅ **Doctrine of Lapse**: Dalhousie; Satara 1848, Jhansi annexed in 1854 after the 1853 succession dispute, Nagpur 1854.
- ✅ **Punjab** annexed after the Second Anglo-Sikh War in **1849**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Subsidiary Alliance = Wellesley; Doctrine of Lapse = Dalhousie. Do not mix authors, dates or mechanisms.

- ❌ Awadh was annexed by Doctrine of Lapse. → Awadh was annexed in **1856** for alleged misgovernance.
- ❌ Tipu died in the Third Anglo-Mysore War. → Tipu died in the **Fourth** war in **1799**.
- ❌ Treaty of Mangalore ended the First Anglo-Mysore War. → It ended the **Second** war.
- ❌ Treaty of Salbai ended the Second Anglo-Maratha War. → It ended the **First** war.
- ❌ Ranjit Singh fought the Anglo-Sikh Wars. → The wars occurred **after his death**.
