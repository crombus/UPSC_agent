## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — the Janata experiment failed, and Indira returned.**

- ✅ The **Janata Party** — a merger of anti-Emergency forces (Congress-O, Jana Sangh, BLD, Socialists) — won in 1977 with **Morarji Desai** as PM, India's first non-Congress government at the Centre.
- ✅ Janata **disintegrated by 1979** over factionalism and the Jana Sangh–RSS "dual membership" issue; **Charan Singh** briefly headed a caretaker government.
- ✅ **Indira Gandhi returned in 1980**; **Sanjay Gandhi died** that June, bringing **Rajiv Gandhi** into politics.
- ⚠️ The interlude showed that anti-Congressism could win an election but could not, by itself, sustain a government.

**Foundation — the 1980s were dominated by regional assertions and crises.**

- ✅ **Regional parties** rose sharply: **AIADMK/DMK** in Tamil Nadu, **Telugu Desam (NTR, 1983)** in Andhra, and the **Left Front (Jyoti Basu)** in West Bengal.
- ✅ The **Assam Movement** (AASU, anti-"foreigner" agitation) convulsed the North-East.
- ✅ In **Jammu & Kashmir**, the politics of the **Abdullahs** (Sheikh, then Farooq) played out against central interference.
- ✅ The **Punjab crisis** — Akali demands (Anandpur Sahib Resolution), the rise of **Bhindranwale**, and militancy — culminated in **Operation Blue Star (1984)** and Indira's assassination.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ The **Janata Party (1977)** formed India's first **non-Congress government** at the Centre; **Morarji Desai** was PM.
- ✅ **Charan Singh** was PM of the caretaker government in **1979**.
- ✅ **Indira Gandhi returned to power in 1980**; **Sanjay Gandhi died** in June 1980.
- ✅ **Telugu Desam** was founded by **N.T. Rama Rao** and swept Andhra Pradesh in **1983**.
- ✅ The **Left Front** under **Jyoti Basu** governed West Bengal from **1977**.
- ✅ The **Anandpur Sahib Resolution** framed Akali demands in Punjab.
- ✅ **Operation Blue Star (June 1984)** preceded Indira Gandhi's assassination on **31 October 1984**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Janata **fell over the "dual membership" issue** (Jana Sangh members' RSS ties), not merely personality clashes.

- ❌ Janata was a single unified party. → It was a **merger of disparate parties** that soon fragmented.
- ❌ Charan Singh led Janata to victory. → He headed a short **caretaker government (1979)** after Desai resigned.
- ❌ Telugu Desam was founded by a Congress leader. → It was founded by film star **N.T. Rama Rao (1983)**.
- ❌ Operation Blue Star was in 1983. → It was in **June 1984**.
- ❌ Indira Gandhi was assassinated during the Emergency. → She was assassinated on **31 October 1984**, years after it ended.
