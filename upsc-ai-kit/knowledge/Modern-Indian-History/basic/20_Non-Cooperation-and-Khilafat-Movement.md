## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — Khilafat gave Gandhi a unity platform.**

- ✅ Indian Muslims feared harsh treatment of the Ottoman Sultan-Caliph after World War I and the Treaty of Sèvres.
- ✅ The Khilafat leadership included the **Ali Brothers** (Maulana Mohammad Ali and Shaukat Ali), Maulana Azad and Hakim Ajmal Khan.
- ✅ Gandhi linked the Khilafat issue with Swaraj to build Hindu-Muslim unity and widen anti-British mobilisation.

**Foundation — Non-Cooperation was the first all-India mass movement.**

- ✅ The movement asked Indians to withdraw cooperation from the colonial state by surrendering titles, boycotting schools, courts, councils and foreign cloth, and promoting swadeshi, khadi, charkha and national education.
- ✅ The **Nagpur Congress** reorganised the Congress with linguistic provincial committees, an AICC, a Working Committee and a low membership fee.
- ✅ Gandhi withdrew the movement after the **Chauri Chaura** violence because he believed the people were not yet disciplined in non-violence.
- ⚠️ The withdrawal disappointed many nationalists but preserved Gandhi's insistence that means and ends must remain consistent.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ The **Khilafat Committee** was formed in **November 1919**.
- ✅ The **Ali Brothers** were Maulana Mohammad Ali and Shaukat Ali.
- ✅ Non-Cooperation was adopted at **Calcutta Special Session (September 1920)** and ratified at **Nagpur (December 1920)**.
- ✅ The programme included surrender of titles, boycott of schools/courts/councils/foreign cloth and promotion of khadi.
- ✅ Nagpur Congress reorganised Congress on **linguistic provincial committee** lines and created a stronger Working Committee structure.
- ✅ **Chauri Chaura** occurred on **5 February 1922**; Gandhi withdrew NCM on **12 February 1922**.
- ✅ Gandhi was arrested in **March 1922** and sentenced to six years.
- ✅ The Caliphate was abolished by **Mustafa Kemal in 1924**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Chauri Chaura caused withdrawal of the movement; it did not start the movement.

- ❌ Khilafat was a Hindu religious movement. → ✅ It was a Muslim political-religious issue linked by Gandhi to national Swaraj.
- ❌ Non-Cooperation began with tax refusal. → ✅ Tax refusal was a later possible stage; early programme focused on boycott and constructive work.
- ❌ Nagpur only passed a political resolution. → ✅ It also reorganised Congress for mass politics.
- ❌ Chauri Chaura was in Punjab. → ✅ It was in **Gorakhpur district**, United Provinces.
- ❌ Gandhi withdrew because the movement was weak. → ✅ It was withdrawn because violence violated the non-violent method.
