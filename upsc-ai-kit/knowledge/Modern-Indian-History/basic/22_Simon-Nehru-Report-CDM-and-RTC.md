## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — constitutional deadlock pushed nationalism from dominion status to complete independence.**

- ✅ The **Simon Commission** was appointed in **1927** and reached India in **1928** without any Indian member.
- ✅ The boycott slogan **"Simon Go Back"** turned the Commission into a nationwide anti-imperialist issue across parties and regions.
- ✅ **Lala Lajpat Rai** was injured in the Lahore lathi-charge and died in **November 1928**.
- ✅ The **Nehru Report (1928)**, prepared by a committee chaired by **Motilal Nehru**, proposed dominion status, fundamental rights and joint electorates.
- ✅ **Jinnah's Fourteen Points (1929)** rejected the Nehru Report; Jawaharlal Nehru and Subhas Bose demanded full independence.

**Foundation — Civil Disobedience converted Purna Swaraj into mass action.**

- ✅ The **Lahore Congress (December 1929)** adopted **Purna Swaraj**; Jawaharlal Nehru presided and the tricolour was hoisted at Ravi.
- ✅ **26 January 1930** was observed as the first nationalist **Independence Day**.
- ✅ Gandhi launched the **Dandi Salt March** on **12 March 1930** and broke the salt law at Dandi on **6 April 1930**.
- ✅ CDM spread through salt satyagraha, foreign cloth/liquor boycott, no-tax campaigns, forest-law defiance, Dharasana, Peshawar and Sholapur.
- ✅ The **Gandhi–Irwin Pact (5 March 1931)** suspended CDM and enabled Congress participation in the Second Round Table Conference.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **Simon Commission** = appointed **1927**, arrived **1928**, no Indian member.
- ✅ **Lala Lajpat Rai** died in **November 1928** after the Lahore lathi-charge during anti-Simon protests.
- ✅ **Nehru Report (1928)** = Motilal Nehru committee; dominion status; fundamental rights; no separate electorates.
- ✅ **Jinnah's Fourteen Points (1929)** responded to the Nehru Report and demanded minority safeguards.
- ✅ **Lahore Congress (Dec 1929)** declared **Purna Swaraj**; **Jawaharlal Nehru** was president.
- ✅ **26 January 1930** was first observed as Independence Day by nationalists.
- ✅ **Dandi March** ran from **12 March to 6 April 1930**, from Sabarmati to Dandi.
- ✅ **Gandhi–Irwin Pact** was signed on **5 March 1931**.
- ✅ **Poona Pact (Sept 1932)** = reserved seats within joint electorates, not separate electorates.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Keep the sequence straight — **Purna Swaraj declared in 1929**, Independence Day observed on **26 January 1930**, and Dandi March began in **March 1930**.

- ❌ Simon Commission included Indian representatives. → ✅ It was an **all-white** commission with no Indian member.
- ❌ Nehru Report demanded complete independence. → ✅ It proposed **dominion status**; Purna Swaraj came at Lahore in 1929.
- ❌ Gandhi represented Congress in the First RTC. → ✅ Congress was absent from the **First RTC**; Gandhi attended the **Second RTC**.
- ❌ Poona Pact accepted separate electorates for Depressed Classes. → ✅ It replaced separate electorates with **reserved seats in joint electorates**.
- ❌ CDM was only a salt protest. → ✅ Salt was the opening symbol; the movement expanded to boycott, no-tax and forest-law defiance.
