## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — revolutionary nationalism grew from repression and impatience.**

- ✅ After the Swadeshi movement declined under repression, some young nationalists turned to individual heroism, secret societies and armed action.
- ✅ They believed that dramatic attacks on officials could inspire courage, avenge repression and awaken the nation.
- ⚠️ The phase is often called revolutionary terrorism in older histories, but the exam-safe phrase is revolutionary nationalism or armed revolutionary activity.
- ✅ It was strongest in Bengal, Maharashtra, Punjab and among overseas Indians in Britain and North America.

**Foundation — scattered actions created a nationalist legend but not a mass uprising.**

- ✅ In Bengal, Anushilan Samiti and Jugantar became centres of revolutionary activity; Barindra Kumar Ghosh and Bhupendranath Datta were associated with this milieu.
- ✅ In Maharashtra, the Chapekar brothers and later V.D. Savarkar's Abhinav Bharat represented armed nationalist currents.
- ✅ Abroad, India House in London, Shyamji Krishna Varma, Madan Lal Dhingra, Madame Cama and the Ghadar Party internationalised anti-colonial politics.
- ⚠️ Revolutionary activity inspired sacrifice and fearlessness but remained limited by secrecy, repression and weak mass organisation.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **Khudiram Bose** and **Prafulla Chaki** were linked with the **Muzaffarpur attack of 1908** targeting Kingsford.
- ✅ **Alipore Bomb Case** followed revolutionary activity in Bengal and involved the Aurobindo-Barindra circle.
- ✅ **Rash Behari Bose** and **Sachin Sanyal** were linked with the **Delhi Conspiracy of 1912** against Hardinge.
- ✅ **Chapekar brothers** assassinated **Rand** in **1897** in Poona.
- ✅ **India House** in London was associated with **Shyamji Krishna Varma**.
- ✅ **Madan Lal Dhingra** killed **Curzon-Wyllie** in **1909** in London.
- ✅ **Ghadar Party** was founded in **1913** in **San Francisco**.
- ✅ **Komagata Maru** incident occurred in **1914**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Match revolutionary names with the correct region and event.

- ❌ Khudiram Bose and Prafulla Chaki belong to the Delhi Conspiracy. → ✅ They belong to the Muzaffarpur attack of 1908.
- ❌ Ghadar began in Bengal in 1905. → ✅ Ghadar was founded in North America, at San Francisco, in 1913.
- ❌ Revolutionary nationalism was the same as Gandhian mass satyagraha. → ⚠️ It relied on secret action and heroic violence, not open mass non-violence.
- ❌ Madame Cama founded the Ghadar Party. → ✅ She was linked with overseas nationalist propaganda and the Stuttgart flag episode, not Ghadar's founding.
