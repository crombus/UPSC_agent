## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — democracy survived two successions and a war.**

- ✅ **Shastri** (PM June 1964–Jan 1966) led India through the **1965 war** and signed the **Tashkent Declaration** with Ayub Khan, dying there in January 1966.
- ✅ **Indira Gandhi** became PM in 1966, initially backed by the Congress bosses (the "**Syndicate**") who expected to control her.
- ✅ The **1967 elections** ended Congress's easy dominance: its Lok Sabha majority shrank and it **lost power in eight states** amid defections and coalitions.
- ⚠️ Each transition tested — and vindicated — the resilience of Indian democracy.

**Foundation — Indira consolidated power by turning left.**

- ✅ Indira **split the Congress in 1969** (Congress-R vs Congress-O), defeating the Syndicate; **V.V. Giri** won the presidency as her candidate.
- ✅ She **nationalised 14 banks (1969)**, **abolished the privy purses (1971)** and campaigned on "**Garibi Hatao**," winning a landslide in **1971**.
- ✅ The **1971 Bangladesh war** ended in a decisive victory and the creation of Bangladesh, making Indira hugely popular.
- ✅ India's first nuclear test, **Pokhran-I**, followed in **May 1974**.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **Shastri** coined "**Jai Jawan Jai Kisan**"; he signed the **Tashkent Declaration (1966)** and died there.
- ✅ **Indira Gandhi** defeated **Morarji Desai 355–169** to become PM in 1966.
- ✅ The **rupee was devalued by ~35.5% in June 1966**.
- ✅ The **1967 elections** reduced the Congress majority and cost it power in **eight states**.
- ✅ **14 banks were nationalised in 1969**; the **Congress split** the same year (R vs O).
- ✅ **V.V. Giri** won the 1969 presidential election as Indira's candidate.
- ✅ The **privy purses were abolished in 1971**; India won the **Bangladesh war (1971)**; **Pokhran-I** was in **1974**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: The **1969 Congress split** (Congress-R vs Congress-O) — not 1967 — is when Indira decisively broke the Syndicate's grip.

- ❌ Shastri died in India. → He died at **Tashkent (1966)**.
- ❌ Indira nationalised banks in 1971. → **Bank nationalisation was 1969** (14 banks).
- ❌ Privy purses were abolished in 1969. → They were **abolished in 1971**.
- ❌ The 1967 election gave Congress a comfortable majority. → Its majority was **slashed** and it lost eight states.
- ❌ Pokhran-I was a weapons test in the 1960s. → It was a **1974** "peaceful nuclear explosion."
