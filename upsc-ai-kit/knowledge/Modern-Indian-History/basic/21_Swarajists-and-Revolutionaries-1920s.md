## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — Swarajists answered the post-NCM vacuum.**

- ✅ After Gandhi withdrew Non-Cooperation, Congress leaders split between **No-changers** and **Pro-changers**.
- ✅ No-changers emphasised constructive work: khadi, national schools, Hindu-Muslim unity and removal of untouchability.
- ✅ Pro-changers, led by **C.R. Das** and **Motilal Nehru**, formed the Swaraj Party to enter councils and obstruct colonial governance from within.
- ✅ The Swarajists won important seats in 1923 and exposed the limits of dyarchy and legislative reforms.

**Foundation — revolutionaries revived with a new ideological tone.**

- ✅ The **HRA** was founded in 1924 by leaders including Sachindranath Sanyal, Ram Prasad Bismil and Jogesh Chatterjee.
- ✅ Kakori in 1925 was a train robbery aimed at raising funds for revolutionary activity; several revolutionaries were executed in 1927.
- ✅ The organisation became **HSRA** in 1928 under revolutionaries such as Bhagat Singh, Chandrashekhar Azad, Sukhdev and Bhagwati Charan Vohra.
- ✅ Bhagat Singh and Batukeshwar Dutt threw bombs in the Central Legislative Assembly in 1929 "to make the deaf hear".

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ The **Swaraj Party** was founded on **1 January 1923** by **C.R. Das** and **Motilal Nehru**.
- ✅ Swarajists followed the policy of **council-entry** and obstruction from within.
- ✅ The **HRA** was founded in **1924**; important names include Sachindranath Sanyal, Ram Prasad Bismil and Jogesh Chatterjee.
- ✅ **Kakori conspiracy** occurred in **1925**; Bismil, Ashfaqulla Khan, Roshan Singh and Rajendra Lahiri were hanged in 1927.
- ✅ HRA became **HSRA in 1928**, marking a socialist turn.
- ✅ **Saunders** was killed in December 1928 to avenge Lala Lajpat Rai's death.
- ✅ Central Assembly Bomb case occurred on **8 April 1929**.
- ✅ Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru were executed on **23 March 1931**.
- ✅ **Chittagong Armoury Raid** occurred in 1930 under Surya Sen.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Keep the sequence HRA 1924 → Kakori 1925 → HSRA 1928 → Assembly Bomb 1929.

- ❌ Swarajists rejected Congress and nationalism. → ✅ They remained within the nationalist stream but changed tactics.
- ❌ No-changers were inactive. → ✅ They focused on constructive work and social reform.
- ❌ HRA and HSRA were identical in ideology. → ✅ HSRA signalled a clearer **socialist republican** orientation.
- ❌ Kakori was led by Bhagat Singh. → ✅ Kakori was associated with Bismil, Ashfaqulla Khan and HRA revolutionaries.
- ❌ Chittagong was in Punjab. → ✅ It was in Bengal, led by Surya Sen.
