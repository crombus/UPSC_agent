## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — Rajiv promised modernity but was undone by scandal and mismanaged identity politics.**

- ✅ **Rajiv Gandhi** won a **record mandate (1984)** and pushed **technology, computerisation, telecom, panchayati raj and education**.
- ✅ He passed the **Anti-Defection Act (1985)** and signed the **Punjab (Rajiv–Longowal), Assam and Mizoram accords**.
- ✅ But the **Shah Bano reversal (1986)** and the **opening of the Ayodhya shrine** alienated both communities, while the **Bofors scandal (1987)** wrecked his "Mr Clean" image.
- ✅ **V.P. Singh** broke away to lead an anti-corruption **National Front**, defeating Congress in **1989**.

**Foundation — 1989–99 was the era of coalitions, Mandal and Mandir.**

- ✅ **V.P. Singh's** government implemented the **Mandal Commission (1990)** — 27% reservation for OBCs — triggering nationwide agitation.
- ✅ **Advani's rath yatra (1990)** intensified the **Ayodhya** mobilisation; the **Babri Masjid was demolished in December 1992**.
- ✅ **Narasimha Rao (1991)** launched **economic liberalisation**; **Rajiv Gandhi was assassinated in 1991**.
- ✅ The decade closed with unstable coalitions — a short **BJP government**, the **United Front** (Deve Gowda, Gujral), and the **NDA under Vajpayee (1998–99)**.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **Rajiv Gandhi** won the **largest-ever majority (1984, ~415 seats)**.
- ✅ The **Anti-Defection Act** was the **52nd Amendment (1985)**.
- ✅ The **Shah Bano** case (1985) and its legislative reversal were a turning point in identity politics.
- ✅ The **Bofors** scandal broke in **1987**.
- ✅ The **Mandal Commission** recommended **27% reservation for OBCs**, implemented in **1990** by **V.P. Singh**.
- ✅ The **Babri Masjid was demolished on 6 December 1992**.
- ✅ **Economic liberalisation** began in **1991** under **Narasimha Rao** (FM Manmohan Singh); **Rajiv Gandhi was assassinated in 1991**.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: **"Mandal" (caste/OBC reservation)** and **"Mandir" (Ayodhya)** were parallel mobilisations of **1990** — don't conflate the two.

- ❌ The Anti-Defection Act was passed under V.P. Singh. → It was the **52nd Amendment (1985)** under **Rajiv Gandhi**.
- ❌ Economic reforms began under Rajiv. → **Liberalisation began in 1991** under **Narasimha Rao**.
- ❌ The Mandal quota was 50%. → It was **27% for OBCs** (within the overall reservation ceiling).
- ❌ The Babri Masjid was demolished in 1990. → It was demolished in **December 1992**.
- ❌ Vajpayee first became PM in 1998. → He led a short government in **1996** before the **NDA government of 1998–99**.
