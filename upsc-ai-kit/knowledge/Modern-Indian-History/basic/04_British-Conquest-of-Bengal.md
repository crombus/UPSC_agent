## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — Bengal was rich, strategic and vulnerable.**

- ✅ Bengal was one of the richest Mughal provinces, with fertile agriculture, textile production and active trade.
- ✅ Murshid Quli Khan, Alivardi Khan and Siraj-ud-Daula made Bengal autonomous in practice while still linked to Mughal legitimacy.
- ✅ Conflict grew from Company misuse of **dastaks** and the **1717 farman**, private trade by Company servants, and unauthorised fortification of Calcutta.
- ⚠️ The "Black Hole" episode is historically contested and should not be used as the main cause of conquest.

**Foundation — Plassey was a coup; Buxar was the decisive military victory.**

- ✅ **Plassey (23 June 1757)** was won by Clive through conspiracy with Mir Jafar, Rai Durlabh, Jagat Seth and others; it was near-bloodless.
- ✅ Mir Jafar became Nawab but failed to satisfy Company demands; Mir Qasim replaced him and tried to reform Bengal from Munger.
- ✅ Mir Qasim abolished inland duties to counter Company privilege, leading to open conflict.
- ✅ **Buxar (22 Oct 1764)** was a decisive battle where Hector Munro defeated Mir Qasim, Shuja-ud-Daula of Awadh and Shah Alam II.
- ✅ The **Treaty of Allahabad (1765)** gave the Company Diwani, turning it into the revenue power of Bengal, Bihar and Orissa.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ **Siraj-ud-Daula** was the Nawab defeated at **Plassey (23 June 1757)**.
- ✅ Plassey was won by conspiracy involving **Mir Jafar**, **Rai Durlabh**, **Jagat Seth** and **Amichand**.
- ✅ **Mir Qasim** shifted his capital to **Munger**, reformed administration and abolished inland duties.
- ✅ **Buxar (22 Oct 1764)**: Hector Munro defeated **Mir Qasim + Shuja-ud-Daula + Shah Alam II**.
- ✅ **Treaty of Allahabad (1765)**: Company got **Diwani of Bengal, Bihar and Orissa**.
- ✅ **Dual Government** lasted from **1765 to 1772** and was ended by **Warren Hastings**.
- ✅ **Bengal Famine of 1770** occurred during the period of Company revenue dominance.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Plassey gave the Company political entry through conspiracy; Buxar gave it military supremacy and legal revenue rights.

- ❌ Plassey was a major battlefield victory. → It was largely a **conspiracy-backed coup**.
- ❌ Buxar was less important than Plassey. → Buxar established **real military supremacy** over Bengal, Awadh and the Mughal emperor.
- ❌ Diwani meant the Company became Nawab. → Diwani meant **revenue rights**; administration nominally remained with the Nawab under Dual Government.
- ❌ Awadh was annexed in 1765. → Awadh was preserved as a **buffer state**.
- ❌ The Black Hole episode alone caused the conflict. → The deeper causes were trade privileges, fortification and Company private trade.
