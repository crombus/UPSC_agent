## PATTERN INSIGHTS (drives file design)
1. **UPSC never asks "write all about school X."** It asks **comparisons, debates, applied scenarios, statement-critiques**.
2. **Cross-school debate is the dominant axis** — Nyaya↔Buddhist, Bhatta↔Prabhakara, Sankara↔Ramanuja↔Madhva, Carvaka↔Nyaya, Mimamsa↔Nyaya.
3. **Pramana-epistemology is the single highest-yield cluster** (perception, anumana, arthapatti, anupalabdhi, smrti, svatah/paratah pramanya, khyativada) — asked every year, multiple questions.
4. **Quote/statement-based prompts** are standard → need a Sanskrit-statement bank with ready interpretations.
5. **Applied/thought-experiment framing** ("candidate who never studies…", "air has no heat…") → answers must show doctrine *applied*, not recited.
6. Vedanta + Nyaya-Vaisesika + Buddhism + Samkhya-Yoga carry the **most marks**; Carvaka/Jaina/Mimamsa/Aurobindo are smaller but **every-year** items.
