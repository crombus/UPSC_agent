# AUROBINDO — Syllabus Item 20

> **Syllabus:** Evolution, Involution; Integral Yoga.
> **Grounding:** ✅ = canonical (Sri Aurobindo, *The Life Divine*; Chatterjee & Datta; Radhakrishnan) · ⚠️ = exam synthesis
> **Nature:** Modern Indian philosopher of **spiritual evolution** — a Vedāntic, life-affirming synthesis reconciling Spirit and Matter (against Advaita's world-denial). Key text: *The Life Divine*.

---

## 0. ONE-SCREEN MAP ⚠️

```
SACCIDĀNANDA (the Absolute)
        │  INVOLUTION (Spirit descends, veils itself)
        ▼
   Supermind (Vijñāna) → Overmind → Mind → Life → MATTER (Spirit fully hidden)
        │
        │  EVOLUTION (Spirit re-emerges, ascends)
        ▼
 Matter → Life → Mind → (gap) → SUPERMIND → Life Divine (Gnostic being)
        bridged by ── INTEGRAL YOGA (aspiration + surrender + descent of Supermind)
```
> 🔑 **Mnemonic — "Involution buries, Evolution unburies"** the same Spirit; Supermind is the *hinge* between the two.

---

## 1. THE ABSOLUTE — Saccidānanda ✅
- Reality = **Saccidānanda** (Existence-Consciousness-Bliss), the same Vedāntic Absolute — but Aurobindo insists it is **dynamic and creative**, not a static, world-denying One.
- Between Saccidānanda and the lower worlds stands the **Supermind (Vijñāna / Truth-Consciousness)** — the crucial *link* principle that holds unity-in-multiplicity and enables real, non-illusory creation. This is Aurobindo's distinctive addition to Vedānta. ✅
- **World is REAL, not māyā:** creation is a genuine self-manifestation of the Divine, not an illusion. (Explicit correction of Śaṃkara's *jagat mithyā*.) ⚠️ key exam contrast.

---

## 2. INVOLUTION (the descent) ✅
- **Involution = the Spirit's self-concealment.** Before anything can evolve, the Divine must first *descend and involve* itself — Saccidānanda progressively veils itself through Supermind → Overmind → Mind → Life → and finally hides *completely* in **Matter** (apparently unconscious).
- Hence Matter is not opposed to Spirit — it is **Spirit self-forgotten**, containing the whole divine potential latent within. ✅
- Involution *logically precedes* and *makes possible* evolution: "Nothing can evolve out of Matter which is not already involved in it."

---

## 3. EVOLUTION (the ascent) ✅
- **Evolution = the progressive re-emergence** of the involved Spirit. Nature ascends: **Matter → Life (plant) → Mind (animal, man)** — each stage the manifestation of a hidden deeper principle.
- Unlike Darwinian evolution (mechanical, physical), Aurobindo's is a **spiritual evolution of consciousness** — the driving force is the involved Divine seeking to manifest, not merely survival. ✅
- **Man is a transitional being, not the end.** Mind is not the summit; beyond mind lies the **Supermind**. The next evolutionary step is the emergence of the **Supramental/Gnostic being** and the **Life Divine** — a divinised existence on earth. ✅
- **Triple transformation:** psychic → spiritual → supramental.

---

## 4. INTEGRAL YOGA (the method) ✅
- The conscious means to *accelerate* evolution — to bring down (invoke the **descent of**) the Supermind into human life and transform the whole being.
- **"Integral" = whole-being + world-affirming:** ✅
  - integrates the classic yogas — **jñāna (knowledge) + bhakti (devotion) + karma (works)** — rather than choosing one;
  - transforms *all* levels (body, life, mind), not just liberating the soul;
  - aims not at escape (individual mokṣa / withdrawal) but at **divine life on earth** — collective, terrestrial transformation.
- **Method:** **aspiration** (upward call) + **rejection** (of the lower nature) + **surrender** (to the Divine Mother/Śakti) → invites the **descent** of higher consciousness.
- ⚠️ **Contrast:** Advaita/Sāṃkhya seek *release from* the world; Aurobindo seeks the *divinisation of* the world — liberation is a *beginning*, not the goal.
