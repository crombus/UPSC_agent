## PAPER-II SOCIO-POLITICAL — CA hooks

### 1. Equality / Justice / Liberty
- 📰 **UCC (Uttarakhand Act 2024, in force Jan 2025)** — test-case for Article 44; equal inheritance for women, bans polygamy/child marriage, registers live-in relationships. Live illustration of **equality vs religious liberty / pluralism** tension. Writ petitions pending on Art. 25 (religious freedom) & privacy. ⚠️ deploy in liberty-vs-equality, Rawls, secularism answers.
- 📰 **Sub-classification of SCs (SC 7-judge bench, 2024)** — states may sub-categorise SC quotas → Rawlsian "difference principle" for the most disadvantaged *within* a group. ⚠️ justice-as-fairness illustration.

### 2. Sovereignty
- ✅ **Federalism friction** (Governor vs elected state govts; SC on timelines for assent) — illustrates **Laski's pluralist sovereignty** vs **Austin's monistic** command theory in the Indian federal context. ⚠️

### 3. Individual & State
- 📰 **Right to privacy (Puttaswamy, 2017) applied to UCC live-in registration & data** — rights vs state accountability. ⚠️
- ✅ **Aadhaar / DPDP Act 2023** — individual vs state; duties & accountability. ⚠️

### 4. Forms of Government
- ✅ **Electoral bonds struck down (SC, Feb 2024)** — transparency & the health of **democracy** (procedural vs substantive). ⚠️ deploy in majoritarianism/democracy answers.

### 5. Political Ideologies (Anarchism, Marxism, Socialism)
- ✅ **Welfare-state schemes vs market reforms** debate — socialism vs liberalisation. ⚠️
- ✅ Left-wing extremism (Naxalism) decline — Marxist praxis vs constitutional democracy. ⚠️

### 6. Humanism / Secularism / Multiculturalism
- 📰 **UCC debate** — secularism as **"principled distance" (Bhargava)** vs uniformity; multiculturalism (Kymlicka/Taylor) vs common civil code. ⚠️ **highest-value CA hook for Sec-A.**
- ✅ **Places of Worship Act litigation** — secularism & religious pluralism in law. ⚠️

### 7. Crime & Punishment
- 📰 **Capital punishment:** SC (2024–25) commuted several death sentences to life, stressing **mitigating circumstances & reformative** approach; questioned hanging vs lethal injection as inhumane. ⚠️ **retributive vs reformative** live illustration.
- ✅ **Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023** replaced IPC — reform of criminal law; sedition reframed. ⚠️
- ✅ **Corruption / PMLA rulings, genocide & R2P** (global conflicts) — mass-violence ethics. ⚠️

### 8. Development & Social Progress
- ✅ **Sen's capability approach** vs GDP — deploy with **NITI Aayog Multidimensional Poverty Index**, welfare delivery, JAM trinity. ⚠️
- ✅ Development-vs-displacement (tribal land, environment clearances) — progress vs justice. ⚠️

### 9. Gender Discrimination
- 📰 **Marital rape** — SC yet to decide (as of 2025); Exception to Sec-63 BNS retained; govt argues it may "destroy the institution of marriage." ⚠️ **autonomy/consent vs tradition** — top gender hook.
- 📰 **UCC equal inheritance/property rights for women** (Uttarakhand). ⚠️
- ✅ **Women's Reservation Act (Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam, 2023)** — empowerment/representation. ⚠️
- ✅ Declining but persistent **sex ratio / foeticide (PC-PNDT Act)**. ⚠️

### 10. Caste — Gandhi vs Ambedkar
- 📰 **Caste census** — SC (2025–26) held caste enumeration is executive policy, no judicial bar; first comprehensive caste census since 1931 proceeding. ⚠️ **Ambedkar's data-for-justice** logic vs Gandhi's reformism. **Top Sec-A CA hook.**
- 📰 **SC sub-classification & "creamy layer" within SC/ST (2024)** — Ambedkar's annihilation project in policy form. ⚠️

## PAPER-II PHILOSOPHY OF RELIGION — CA hooks

### Problem of Evil / Suffering
- ✅ Natural disasters, pandemics, wars → live "problem of evil"; Hick's soul-making vs karma theodicy. ⚠️

### Religion & Morality / Religion without God
- ✅ **Ethics debates (euthanasia — SC passive euthanasia guidelines, living wills 2018/2023)** — autonomy of morality from religion (Euthyphro). ⚠️
- ✅ Rise of "spiritual but not religious" / secular ethics — **religion without God** (Buddhism, Braithwaite). ⚠️

### Religious Pluralism & Absolute Truth
- ✅ **Communal harmony / inter-faith tension** — Hick's pluralism, *"ekaṃ sat viprā bahudhā vadanti"* as India's constitutional-secular ideal. ⚠️ deploy in pluralism + secularism answers.

### Religious Language / Experience
- ⚠️ Debates on faith vs scientific rationality, "scientific temper" (Art. 51A) — reason/revelation/faith. ⚠️
