## PAPER-II SOCIO-POLITICAL — CA hooks

### 1. Equality / Justice / Liberty
- 📰 **UCC (Uttarakhand Act 2024, in force 27 Jan 2025)** — test-case for Article 44; uniform rules on marriage, divorce and succession, with mandatory registration of live-in relationships. The Code remains operational while privacy and religious-freedom objections are litigated. ⚠️ deploy in liberty-vs-equality, Rawls and secularism answers.
- 📰 **Sub-classification of SCs (SC 7-judge bench, 2024)** — states may sub-categorise SC quotas → Rawlsian "difference principle" for the most disadvantaged *within* a group. ⚠️ justice-as-fairness illustration.

### 2. Sovereignty
- 📰 **Governor's assent and federalism (2025 Presidential Reference)** — the Supreme Court rejected court-created fixed timelines and "deemed assent", while leaving prolonged, unexplained inaction open to limited judicial scrutiny. Illustrates **Laski's pluralist sovereignty** vs **Austin's monistic** command theory. ⚠️

### 3. Individual & State
- 📰 **Right to privacy (Puttaswamy, 2017) applied to UCC live-in registration & data** — rights vs state accountability. ⚠️
- ✅ **Aadhaar / DPDP Act 2023** — individual vs state; duties & accountability. ⚠️

### 4. Forms of Government
- ✅ **Electoral bonds struck down (SC, Feb 2024)** — transparency & the health of **democracy** (procedural vs substantive). ⚠️ deploy in majoritarianism/democracy answers.

### 5. Political Ideologies (Anarchism, Marxism, Socialism)
- ✅ **Welfare-state schemes vs market reforms** debate — socialism vs liberalisation. ⚠️
- ✅ Left-wing extremism (Naxalism) decline — Marxist praxis vs constitutional democracy. ⚠️

### 6. Humanism / Secularism / Multiculturalism
- 📰 **UCC debate** — secularism as **"principled distance" (Bhargava)** vs uniformity; multiculturalism (Kymlicka/Taylor) vs common civil code. ⚠️ **highest-value CA hook for Sec-A.**
- 📰 **Places of Worship Act litigation** — constitutional challenges remain pending. The Supreme Court's December 2024 order barred registration of fresh religious-character suits and, in pending suits, barred effective interim/final orders (including survey directions) until further orders. Use for secularism, historical justice and pluralism. ⚠️

### 7. Crime & Punishment
- 📰 **Capital punishment:** mitigation and the possibility of reform remain central to death-sentence review. A separate challenge to hanging as the mode of execution remains pending; hanging is still the prescribed method as of 2026-07. ⚠️ **retributive vs reformative** live illustration.
- ✅ **Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), 2023** replaced the IPC from 1 Jul 2024; Section 152 addresses acts endangering sovereignty, unity and integrity rather than reproducing IPC Section 124A verbatim. ⚠️
- ✅ **Corruption / PMLA rulings, genocide & R2P** (global conflicts) — mass-violence ethics. ⚠️

### 8. Development & Social Progress
- ✅ **Sen's capability approach** vs GDP — deploy with **NITI Aayog Multidimensional Poverty Index**, welfare delivery, JAM trinity. ⚠️
- ✅ Development-vs-displacement (tribal land, environment clearances) — progress vs justice. ⚠️

### 9. Gender Discrimination
- 📰 **Marital rape** — Exception 2 to BNS Section 63 still excludes non-consensual sexual acts by a husband against an adult wife from the rape definition; constitutional challenges remain pending as of 2026-07. ⚠️ **autonomy/consent vs tradition** — top gender hook.
- 📰 **UCC equal inheritance/property rights for women** (Uttarakhand). ⚠️
- ✅ **Women's Reservation Act (Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam, 2023)** — representation and substantive equality; commencement is linked to a post-census delimitation exercise. ⚠️
- ✅ Declining but persistent **sex ratio / foeticide (PC-PNDT Act)**. ⚠️

### 10. Caste — Gandhi vs Ambedkar
- 📰 **Caste enumeration in Census 2027** — the notified two-phase Census includes caste enumeration; reference dates are 1 Oct 2026 for specified snow-bound areas and 1 Mar 2027 for the rest of India. It will be the first population Census since 1931 to enumerate caste beyond SC/ST categories. ⚠️ **Ambedkar's data-for-justice** logic vs Gandhi's reformism. **Top Sec-A CA hook.**
- ✅ **SC sub-classification (State of Punjab v. Davinder Singh, 2024)** — the seven-judge bench permitted sub-classification within Scheduled Castes to reach the more disadvantaged. Separate opinions discussed a creamy-layer principle, but the judgment did not itself create a uniform exclusion rule. ⚠️

## PAPER-II PHILOSOPHY OF RELIGION — CA hooks

### Problem of Evil / Suffering
- ✅ Natural disasters, pandemics, wars → live "problem of evil"; Hick's soul-making vs karma theodicy. ⚠️

### Religion & Morality / Religion without God
- ✅ **Ethics debates (euthanasia — SC passive euthanasia guidelines, living wills 2018/2023)** — autonomy of morality from religion (Euthyphro). ⚠️
- ✅ Rise of "spiritual but not religious" / secular ethics — **religion without God** (Buddhism, Braithwaite). ⚠️

### Religious Pluralism & Absolute Truth
- ✅ **Communal harmony / inter-faith tension** — Hick's pluralism, *"ekaṃ sat viprā bahudhā vadanti"* as India's constitutional-secular ideal. ⚠️ deploy in pluralism + secularism answers.

### Religious Language / Experience
- ⚠️ Debates on faith vs scientific rationality, "scientific temper" (Art. 51A) — reason/revelation/faith. ⚠️
