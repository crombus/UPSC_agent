# THINKER-vs-THINKER COMPARISON MATRICES — the exam's favourite format

> **Use:** UPSC loves "compare / distinguish / contrast." These grids are answer-ready skeletons. ✅ canonical · ⚠️ exam-synthesis.

---

## 1. RATIONALISTS — Descartes vs Spinoza vs Leibniz
| Axis | Descartes | Spinoza | Leibniz |
|---|---|---|---|
| Substance | **two** (mind, matter) + God | **one** (God/Nature) | **infinite** monads |
| God | perfect being, guarantor | = Nature (pantheism) | supreme monad, creator |
| Mind–body | **interaction** (pineal gland) | **parallelism** (two attributes) | **pre-established harmony** |
| Freedom | libertarian free will | strict determinism | compatibilist (best world) |
| Method | doubt → cogito | geometric deduction | logic / sufficient reason |
> 🔑 "**1 substance splits (Descartes 2) → collapses to 1 (Spinoza) → explodes to ∞ (Leibniz).**"

## 2. EMPIRICISTS — Locke vs Berkeley vs Hume
| Axis | Locke | Berkeley | Hume |
|---|---|---|---|
| Matter | exists (support of qualities) | **denied** (esse est percipi) | unknowable |
| Qualities | primary/secondary distinction | all secondary (mind-dependent) | — |
| Self | thinking substance | spiritual substance | **bundle** (no substance) |
| God | provable | perceiver sustaining world | scepticism |
| Result | representative realism | idealism | scepticism |
> 🔑 "**Empiricism eats itself:** Locke keeps matter → Berkeley eats matter → Hume eats the self & causation."

## 3. KANT — the hinge ⚠️
| Problem | Hume's endpoint | Kant's answer |
|---|---|---|
| Causation | mere habit | a priori **category** |
| Synthetic a priori | impossible | **possible** (maths, science) |
| Self | bundle | transcendental unity of apperception |
| Metaphysics | sceptical dead-end | limited to phenomena; noumena unknowable |
> 🔑 Kant = "**I awoke from dogmatic slumber**" — answers Hume by making the mind *constitute* experience.

## 4. VEDĀNTA — Śaṅkara vs Rāmānuja vs Madhva
| Axis | Śaṅkara (Advaita) | Rāmānuja (Viśiṣṭādvaita) | Madhva (Dvaita) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brahman–world | non-dual; world = **māyā** | qualified non-dual; world real, body of God | **dualism**; world & God distinct |
| Jīva–Brahman | identical (māyā veils) | part/mode of Brahman (aprthaksiddhi) | eternally **different** (pañcavidhabheda) |
| Brahman | nirguṇa (attributeless) | saguṇa (with attributes) | saguṇa, personal (Viṣṇu) |
| Mokṣa | knowledge (jñāna); merger | devotion; communion | devotion; service, hierarchy |
| Error | adhyāsa (superimposition) | satkhyāti (partial real) | abhinava-anyathākhyāti |
> 🔑 "**Identity → Part → Difference**" (Śaṅkara → Rāmānuja → Madhva).

## 5. SOVEREIGNTY — Austin vs Bodin vs Laski vs Kautilya
| Thinker | Sovereignty is… | Type |
|---|---|---|
| **Bodin** | supreme, perpetual power of the state; but under divine/natural law | early absolutist |
| **Austin** | command of a determinate superior, habitually obeyed | **monistic/legal** |
| **Laski** | no absolute sovereign; power **pluralist**, shared with associations | **pluralist** |
| **Kautilya** | king's authority via *daṇḍa* (rod), bound by *dharma* & welfare | statecraft/ethical |
> 🔑 "Bodin bounds it, Austin absolutises it, Laski disperses it, Kautilya *dharma*-tises it."

## 6. CAUSATION — across Indian schools ✅
| View | Effect pre-exists in cause? | Schools |
|---|---|---|
| **Asatkāryavāda** (ārambhavāda) | NO — new production | Nyāya-Vaiśeṣika |
| **Satkāryavāda — pariṇāmavāda** | YES — real transformation | Sāṃkhya-Yoga |
| **Satkāryavāda — vivartavāda** | YES — apparent/illusory change | Advaita |
| **Pratītyasamutpāda** | dependent arising (no substance) | Buddhism |
> 🔑 "Does the pot pre-exist in the clay? Nyāya: No · Sāṃkhya: Yes-really · Advaita: Yes-apparently · Buddhism: neither (dependent)."

## 7. CASTE — Gandhi vs Ambedkar (single most-repeated Paper-II clash) ✅
| Axis | Gandhi | Ambedkar |
|---|---|---|
| Varṇa system | reform, retain as division of labour (no hierarchy) | **abolish** — root of oppression |
| Untouchability | evil, must go ("Harijan") | symptom; caste itself must go |
| Method | change of heart, trusteeship | **law, rights, conversion** |
| Religion | reform Hinduism from within | left Hinduism → Buddhism |
| Slogan | uplift of Harijans | **"Annihilation of Caste"** |
> 🔑 "**Gandhi HEALS Hinduism; Ambedkar ANNIHILATES caste.**"

## 8. SELF — across traditions ⚠️
| Tradition | Self is… |
|---|---|
| Descartes | thinking substance (res cogitans) |
| Hume | a bundle of perceptions |
| Nyāya | eternal substance, substrate of cognition |
| Sāṃkhya | puruṣa — pure witness consciousness |
| Advaita | ātman = Brahman (only reality) |
| Buddhism | **anātman** — no permanent self |
| Strawson | "person" — logically primitive, bearer of both M- & P-predicates |
> 🔑 Bridges Paper-I Western + Indian; deploy in any "nature of self" question.
