## 1. Snapshot & core idea

**Foundation — why Mauryan rule was a landmark.**

✅ RS Sharma treats Mauryan rule as important because it expanded state control, taxation, economic regulation, stone art and material culture across large areas.
- ✅ The Maurya period was a landmark in taxation: Kautilya names many taxes collected from peasants, artisans and traders.
- ✅ A strong machinery was needed for assessment, collection and storage.
- ✅ State control and economic regulation were central features of Mauryan rule.
- ✅ The state system and material culture of the Gangetic basin spread to outlying regions.

**Standard — art, architecture and decline.**

✅ The Mauryas made a remarkable contribution to art and architecture by introducing **stone masonry on a wide scale**.
- ✅ Megasthenes compared the Mauryan palace at Pataliputra with the splendour of Iran's capital.
- ✅ Ashokan pillars are famous for their **shining polish**, comparable to NBPW polish.
- ✅ Pillars were mounted by animal capitals; the **Sarnath lion capital** became India's national emblem.
- ✅ After Ashoka's death in **232 BC**, the empire began to disintegrate.
- ✅ RS Sharma lists causes such as Brahmanical reaction, financial pressure, oppressive rule, new knowledge in outlying areas and neglect of the north-west frontier.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Mauryas introduced **stone masonry on a wide scale**.
- ✅ Ashokan pillars have a famous **shining polish**.
- ✅ The **Sarnath lion capital** is the source of India's national emblem.
- ✅ Mauryan taxation covered peasants, artisans and traders.
- ✅ The empire began disintegrating after Ashoka's death in **232 BC**.
- ✅ Post-Mauryan regional states rose partly because material culture had spread beyond Magadha.
- ✅ RS Sharma's decline causes include Brahmanical reaction, financial pressure, oppressive rule and north-west neglect.

## 5. UPSC Traps

> 🔑 Trap: Decline is not one-line; Mauryan significance is not only Ashoka.

- ❌ Mauryan importance lies only in Ashoka's Dhamma. → Also state control, taxation, art, material culture and administration.
- ❌ Ashokan pillars were made of brick. → They were monolithic/polished stone pillars.
- ❌ The Sarnath lion capital is Gupta art. → It is Mauryan/Ashokan.
- ❌ The Mauryan Empire fell immediately because of one cause. → RS Sharma presents multiple causes.
- ❌ Regional states after Mauryas prove total collapse of culture. → They also show spread of material culture and state forms.
