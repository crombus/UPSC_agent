# Vulcanism & Earthquakes — BASIC

> **Subject:** Geography · **Tier:** Basic (foundation) · **GS-I + GS-III**

## Snapshot
| Concept | Fact |
|---|---|
| Magma vs lava | **Magma** = underground; **lava** = on surface |
| Basic lava | Hot, fluid, **quiet**, flows far → **shield volcano** (Hawaii) |
| Acid lava | Viscous, **explosive** → steep cone / plug (Mt. Pelée) |
| Intrusive | **Sill, dyke, batholith, laccolith** (cool at depth) |
| Extrusive | **Lava plateau** (Deccan), cones, **caldera** (Lake Toba) |
| Earthquake | **Focus** (underground) vs **Epicentre** (surface) |

## Core idea
**Vulcanism** = molten material moving to/near the surface, building **intrusive** (underground) and **extrusive**
(surface) landforms. **Earthquakes** = shaking from sudden crustal movement, mostly along **faults** at **plate
boundaries**.

> 🔑 **Basic lava = Broad & Benign** (shield); **Acid lava = Angry & steep** (explosive cone).
> 🔑 **Focus is deep, Epicentre is on top.**
> 🔑 **S-waves Stop at liquids** → proves the **outer core is liquid**.
> 🔑 **Richter = magnitude; Mercalli = damage.**
> 🔑 Composite/strato = **layers** of lava + ash (Fuji, Vesuvius, Etna).

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Volcanoes: **active / dormant / extinct** — "extinct" can revive.
- ✅ **Deccan Plateau** = basalt lava plateau (basic lava).
- ✅ India's seismic zones: **V (highest)** → **II (lowest)**; NE India, Kashmir, Kutch = Zone V.
- ✅ **Barren Island (Andaman)** = India's **only active volcano**; **Narcondam** = dormant.

## 📰 Current link
📰 **Myanmar quake (Mar 2025, M7.7, Sagaing Fault)** shook NE India. **Barren Island erupted (Sept 2025)** after a
nearby earthquake — India's only active volcano. Both sit on the active **India–Burma plate boundary**.
