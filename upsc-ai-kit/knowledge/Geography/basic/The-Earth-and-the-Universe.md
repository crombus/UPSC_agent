## Core idea
The Earth is one of **8 planets** orbiting the **Sun**. It **rotates** west-to-east (→ day & night) and **revolves**
around the Sun (→ seasons). Seasons happen because of the **23½° axial tilt**, **not** distance from the Sun. The
Earth is not a perfect sphere but an **oblate spheroid** (bulging at the Equator).

> 🔑 **8 planets:** *My Very Educated Mother Just Served Us Nachos.*
> 🔑 **Inner (rocky):** Mercury-Venus-Earth-Mars · **Outer (gas giants):** Jupiter-Saturn-Uranus-Neptune.
> 🔑 **Seasons = axial tilt**, not distance. (Earth is actually **closest to the Sun in January** — perihelion.)
> 🔑 Rotation = **15° per hour** = **4 min per 1° longitude**.

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ **Venus** = hottest planet & "Earth's twin"; **Jupiter** = largest; **Saturn** = rings; **Mercury** = smallest/closest.
- ✅ **Asteroid belt** = between **Mars and Jupiter**.
- ✅ Key parallels: Equator **0°**, Tropic of Cancer **23½°N**, Capricorn **23½°S**, Arctic **66½°N**, Antarctic **66½°S**.
- ✅ **IST = 82½°E = GMT + 5:30**. Solstices: **21 June** & **22 Dec**; Equinoxes: **21 Mar** & **23 Sept**.
- ✅ **Solar eclipse = new moon**; **lunar eclipse = full moon**.
