## Core idea
The Earth has three shells — **crust, mantle, core**. Its rocks come in **three families**: **igneous** (from cooled
magma), **sedimentary** (from layered sediments, hold **fossils**), and **metamorphic** (old rocks changed by **heat &
pressure**).

> 🔑 **Fossils only in sedimentary rocks** (igneous & metamorphic have none).
> 🔑 **Granite = intrusive** (slow, big crystals) · **Basalt = extrusive** (fast, fine) → Deccan Traps.
> 🔑 **Metamorphism:** limestone→**marble**, sandstone→**quartzite**, shale→**slate**, coal→**graphite/diamond**.
> 🔑 Discontinuities: **Moho** (crust-mantle) · **Gutenberg** (mantle-core).

## Must-Know Facts
- ✅ Oceanic crust **thin (~5–10 km)**, continental crust **thick (up to ~70 km)**.
- ✅ **Acidic** rocks (granite) = silica-rich, light; **Basic** (basalt) = dark, denser.
- ✅ Sedimentary types: **mechanical** (sandstone), **organic** (limestone, coal), **chemical** (rock salt, gypsum).
- ✅ Outer core liquid + Earth's rotation → the **magnetic field**.
