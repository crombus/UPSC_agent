## 1. Island groups of India

✅ India has island groups in both the **Bay of Bengal** and the **Arabian Sea**. ✅ The Bay of Bengal group is the **Andaman and Nicobar Islands**; the Arabian Sea group is **Lakshadweep**. ✅ The source extraction notes that the Andamans are linked with the **Arakan Yoma** fold chain, while Lakshadweep is coral/atoll in origin.

> 🔑 Trap: Do not treat all Indian islands as coral. Andaman, Nicobar and Lakshadweep have different origins.

## 3. Core static theory

### 3.1 Andaman & Nicobar

✅ The Andaman Islands are described as an extension of the **Tertiary Arakan Yoma** fold chain. ✅ Main rocks listed are sandstone, limestone and shale. ✅ The region lies in a major earthquake zone. ✅ Barren Island and Narcondam lie east of the Andamans in the source notes; **Barren** is active, **Narcondam** dormant.

### 3.2 Lakshadweep

✅ Lakshadweep is in the Arabian Sea and is entirely of **coral origin**. ✅ Its atolls illustrate the coral-reef sequence linked with Darwin's subsidence theory in the basic chapter.

### 3.3 Dynamic coastlines and island fragility

✅ Khullar's extracted notes mention dynamic island coastlines near the Ganga delta, where islands appear and vanish with sea-level/coastal change (e.g. New Moore Island). ⚠️ For UPSC, island geography must be linked to tectonic risk, coral ecology, sea-level change and strategic location.

## 4. Must-Know Facts (Prelims)

- ✅ Andaman = Arakan Yoma extension; tectonic/fold-belt link.
- ✅ Nicobar = mainly coral origin; 18 islands, 11 inhabited in source notes.
- ✅ Barren Island = India's only active volcano.
- ✅ Narcondam = dormant volcano.
- ✅ Lakshadweep = entirely coral atolls in Arabian Sea.
- ✅ A&N region lies in a major earthquake zone.
- ✅ Saddle Peak in North Andaman is ~737 m.

## 5. UPSC Traps

- ❌ Lakshadweep is volcanic like Barren Island → Lakshadweep is **coral/atoll**; Barren Island is volcanic.
- ❌ Andaman Islands are coral like Lakshadweep → Andamans are linked to the **Arakan Yoma fold chain**.
- ❌ Narcondam is India's active volcano → **Barren Island** is active; Narcondam is dormant.
- ❌ Bay of Bengal and Arabian Sea islands have the same origin → Bay islands are tectonic/volcanic/coral mix; Lakshadweep is coral.
- ❌ Island geography is only physical → Indian islands also have strategic, ecological and disaster-risk dimensions.

## 6. 📰 Current link

### CA Anchor — Great Nicobar (Galathea Bay) Mega-Project

📰 **News trigger:** The Great Nicobar mega-project plans an international transshipment port at Galathea Bay plus a township, airport and power plant — pitting strategic/economic gains against a fragile island ecosystem.

✅ The project shows the tension between the Blue Economy/strategic connectivity and conserving a biodiversity-rich, tectonically active coral-and-rainforest island.

- ✅ Components: Galathea Bay transshipment port, greenfield airport, township and power plant on Great Nicobar.
- ✅ Ecological concerns: dredging/reclamation threaten coral reefs, leatherback turtle nesting, coconut crabs and endemic species; the area is seismically active.
- ✅ Governance angle: Environmental Impact Assessment, CRZ clearances and tribal (Shompen/Nicobarese) rights.

### Current-link Must-Know Facts

- ✅ Galathea Bay = proposed transshipment port on Great Nicobar.
- ✅ Threatens coral reefs + leatherback turtle nesting sites.
- ✅ Strategic value: near international shipping lanes / Malacca approaches.

### Current-link Traps

- ❌ Great Nicobar is ecologically insignificant → It hosts coral reefs, leatherback nesting beaches and endemic species in a seismic zone.
