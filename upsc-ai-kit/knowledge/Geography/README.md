## How this folder works

- **Two tiers per topic:** `basic/NN` gives the world/general-concept foundation (definitions,
  models/theories, world patterns and classification); `advanced/NN` gives the India-application
  layer (India-specific data, institutions, current policy and a fuller Mains-answer
  architecture).
- **Same number = same topic** across both tiers (e.g., `basic/26_...md` pairs with
  `advanced/26_...md`).
- **Tagging:** ✅ = directly grounded in a cited source (book excerpt or official/fetched page);
  ⚠️ = analytical inference or standard synthesis; 📰 = a verified, dated current-affairs anchor.
- **Answer discipline:** every dated fact carries its source and date. Book-sourced statistics
  keep the book's own reference date and are never represented as current-year data; where a
  newer official figure supersedes a book figure, the newer source is cited separately and
  dated.
- **No fabrication:** no invented statistics, dates, scheme names, boundary details or PYQ
  question numbers appear in this folder. Where a figure could not be verified, it has been
  omitted or explicitly flagged with a verification caution rather than estimated.

## Verified current anchors and cautions carried across this folder

- ⚠️ **Note on seismic zoning:** official BIS material currently and publicly supports a
  four-zone (Zone II-V) seismic classification for India; no official Zone VI has been
  verified, and this folder does not assert one (Topic 03).
- ⚠️ **Note on Census currency:** India's last completed decennial Census was 2011; any
  population/migration/urbanisation/linguistic/religious figures newer than 2011 are drawn from
  named interim sources (e.g., SRS Bulletins, UN World Population Prospects, NITI Aayog/MoSPI
  survey data) and are dated accordingly — this folder does not assume a later full Census
  exists (Topics 26-29, 35, 37).
- 📰 Dated current-affairs anchors specific to each 2026-relevant topic (e.g., heatwave/monsoon
  dynamics, Great Nicobar development, industrial-corridor and space-programme milestones) are
  carried inside the individual topic files' "Current link" sections with their own source and
  date rather than restated here.
- 📰 **Census 2027 is scheduled, not completed:** the general reference date is
  1 March 2027 (1 October 2026 for specified snow-bound areas). Census 2011
  remains the latest completed all-India baseline at this cutoff.
