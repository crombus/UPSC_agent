Easy Commands You Can Use Later

For Saturday weekly revision:

Create my weekly UPSC revision bundle for this week.

Use Sunday-to-Saturday, Asia/Kolkata.

Inputs:
I am uploading my studied PDFs/notes.

Outputs:
1. Prelims GS1 Question Paper PDF
2. Prelims CSAT Question Paper PDF
3. Mains + Interview Question Paper PDF
4. Answer Key + Explanations PDF
5. Revision Notebook PDF

Default counts:
50 GS1 MCQs
20 CSAT MCQs
10 Mains questions
8 Interview questions

Difficulty:
UPSC standard to slightly above UPSC standard.

For a monthly bundle:

Create my UPSC revision bundle for May 2026.

Outputs:
1. Prelims GS1 Question Paper PDF
2. Prelims CSAT Question Paper PDF
3. Mains + Interview Question Paper PDF
4. Answer Key + Explanations PDF
5. Revision Notebook PDF

Use source verification where available. If I upload PDFs, treat them as the primary source base.

For answer evaluation from paper screenshots:

Evaluate my attempted answers.

I am uploading screenshots/photos or a PDF containing screenshots of my answer sheet.

Use:
- GS1: +2, -0.33, 0 for unattempted
- CSAT: +2.5, -0.83, 0 for unattempted

If any answer is unclear, mark it Unclear and ask me to confirm before final scoring.
